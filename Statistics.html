    Country Name : India

    In 2022, India's healthcare spending was over 2% of its GDP, up from 1.3% in 2020.

    In 2020, the healthcare sector was worth about $280 billion, and was expected to reach $372 billion by 2022.

    India's healthcare industry is one of the country's biggest contributors to jobs and revenue.

    The hospital sector in India was worth INR 7940.87 billion in 2021, and is expected to reach INR 18,348.78 billion by 2027.

    India's healthcare system is organized into three levels: primary, secondary, and tertiary.

    India's healthcare system has a doctor-to-population ratio of 1:2148.
